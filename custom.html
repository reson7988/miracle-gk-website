Custom Workflow定制流程

Our proven 5-step process ensures your vision becomes a reality.我们成熟的5步流程确保您的创意变为现实。

Concept Design概念设计

Share your idea, reference images, or concept art with us. Our design team will work with you to refine the concept and create initial sketches for approval.向我们分享您的创意、参考图或概念原画。我们的设计团队将与您合作完善概念，并制作初始草图供确认。

3D Modeling & Printing3D建模与打印

Once the concept is approved, we create high-precision 3D digital models and produce physical prototypes using professional-grade 3D printers for verification.概念确认后，我们创建高精度3D数字模型，并使用专业级3D打印机生产物理原型供验证。

Mold Making模具制作

After prototype confirmation, we produce high-quality silicone and resin molds to prepare for mass casting. Multiple mold sets ensure production efficiency.原型确认后，我们制作高质量硅胶模和树脂模具，为批量浇铸做准备。多套模具确保生产效率。

Production & QC生产与质检

Vacuum degassing casting, hand finishing, multi-layer painting, and strict quality control at every stage to ensure museum-grade quality for every piece.真空脱泡浇铸、手工修模、多层涂装，每个阶段都经过严格质量控制，确保每件作品达到博物馆级品质。
